**Analyst's Pyramid**


                 INVEST?
                    ▲
              Valuation
                    ▲
        Business Quality
                    ▲
     Financial Statements
                    ▲
          Accounting


Every great investor builds from the bottom up.

1. Learn accounting.
2. Understand the financial statements.
3. Analyze the business.
4. Estimate its value.
5. Then decide whether it's a good investment.



Before Investing every analyst asks these 10 questions:
1. Is the Revenue growing?
2. Are Profits growing?
3. Is Cash Flow strong?
4. Debt?
5. what are Margins doing?
6. Why are customers buying?
7. What risks does the company face?
8. Is the Management trustworthy?
9. Is the company Over Priced?
10. Would I be happy owning this Business for 10 years?
