## What I learned

RSI is built from average gains and losses over a rolling window rather than being a magic indicator.

MACD compares two exponential moving averages to measure momentum.

The histogram represents the distance between MACD and the Signal Line, making momentum changes easier to visualize.

Professional dashboards organize indicators into separate panels instead of displaying everything on one chart.
